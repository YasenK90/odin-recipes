Pasta Recipe

Description:

This pasta recipe is simple and delicious, perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat marinara sauce in a pan.
  3. Drain pasta and mix with olive oil and marinara sauce.
  4. Serve topped with grated Parmesan cheese.
