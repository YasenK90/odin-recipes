Salad Recipe

Description:

This salad recipe is fresh and healthy, perfect for a light lunch or dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 200g mixed salad greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine mixed salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle dressing over salad and toss to combine.
  4. Serve immediately.
