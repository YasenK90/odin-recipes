Tomato Soup Recipe

Description:

This tomato soup recipe is comforting and easy to make, perfect for a cozy night in.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add diced tomatoes and vegetable broth, and bring to a simmer.
  3. Stir in heavy cream and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil if desired.
